Tekken 7

Tekken 7 is a 3D fighting game, where you pick a character from a pool of characters and fight other people either online or offline.
Let's get to know the control scheme, there's the typical forward, crouch, jump, and backward, to dash or side-step tap the movement key twice, and 4 attacks(left and right punch, left and right kick), but is more widely known as 1,2,3 or 4.

Hit propeties

There are some special properties like counter hit, punish, and armor.
Counter hit is when your attack managed to hit while the enemy is pressing a button, and depending on the moves it may know down or launch for heavy damage.
Armor moves are moves that cannot be stopped and will power through all attacks, although they still take damage.

2D vs 3D

As you know tekken 7 is a 3D fighting game, which is different from more popular guys like street fighter which is mostly 2d or 2.5d more specifically.
Although 2D is more common and popular compared to 3d, both have their key differences and it's entirely based on your prefereances.
The main difference is that in 3d games you can move back, forward, and side-step,thus it created an added layer of difficulty which I will dive deeper into later on.
Another big difference is the lack of air-game, jumping is gone from the game and with that the skill of doing an anti-air which is a key fundamental in most 2d fighting game.
